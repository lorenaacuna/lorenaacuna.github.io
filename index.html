Lorena Acuña

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the APEx group (Atmospheric Physics of Exoplanets) in the Max Planck Institute for Astronomy, since December 2022. In my research, I focus on the internal structures and atmospheres of exoplanets, developing modelling tools to interpret mass, radius and atmospheric data from missions and telescopes such as TESS, CHEOPS, ESPRESSO and JWST.
